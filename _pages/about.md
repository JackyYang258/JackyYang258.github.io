Hello! I'm **Siqi Yang** (pronounced /schee/, 杨思琪 in Chinese). I am currently pursuing my **MSCS** at the [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC)](https://illinois.edu/), where I am fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Yuxiong Wang](https://yxw.cs.illinois.edu/).<br><br>My research focuses on Reinforcement Learning for developing agents that can interact naturally with humans, including both embodied agents and LLM-based agents. I am also interested in how these agents build an understanding of the world through 3D vision and other perceptual modalities.<br><br>Before joining UIUC, I completed my B.Eng in Electronic Engineering at [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/). During my undergraduate studies, I gained valuable research experience working with [Prof. Huazhe Xu](http://hxu.rocks/) and [Prof. Yu Wang](https://web.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/wangyu/en/index.htm).
